ORDERING PROCESS

Stunning software is our specialty, as such ordering this software should be as easy as using it. Our simple purchasing model is described below to ensure that purchasing one of our products is as simple as possible!

* All transactions on Compile Projects are subject to the purchasing policy agreement between Compile Projects and its customers.


PRODUCTS


PURCHASING POLICIES


1

ADD TO CART

Find the items you're looking for and add them to cart!

2

CREATE ACCOUNT

Create a Compile Projects Account or login with an existing Compile Projects Account. This is how we'll be able to contact you for further information with regards to your purchase.

3

CHECKOUT / DOWNPAYMENT

Every purchase is subjected to a 20% downpayment for its creation to begin. Proceed to checkout to place an order for your products. An employee will contact you via your email after this is done

4

CREATION OF ITEMS

Upon completion of the previous steps, creation of your software product is commenced.

* Completion and delivery times are based on current cue and may fluctuate. Please feel free to contact us for inquires about the estimated delivery date of your product.

5

FINAL PAYMENT

When completion of your software product has arrived you will be notified via email to make the final payment of 80% of the cost for your product.

* Compile Projects holds the right to not deliver a product in circumstances where the customer refuses to pay the final 80% of the total cost of their product.

CONGRATULATIONS!
