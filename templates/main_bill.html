AMAZING APPLICATIONS... FOR FREE

From its inception, Receipt Maker was intended to be a free web application for anyone around the globe who ran a business but had no way to create beautiful invoices for customer purchases. Despite its stigmatism as a "free web application" it's design incorporates all of the power user features found in our competitors' applications but for free. Logically, you may be asking why? The answer is really simple, we could not find a free way to generate receipts ourselves and wanted to make the world a better place by empowering people with amazing applications for free.

SIMPLE INTERFACE, ENDLESS CUSTOMIZATION
